**Role:** Software Engineer (Spring 2023) / Software Engineer Lead (Summer 2023 – Spring 2024)  
**Competitions:** MATE ROV Competition, VRX 2023 Competition (2nd Place)

As a Software Engineer in Spring 2023, I worked on the embedded systems for the MATE ROV competition. From Summer 2023 to Spring 2024, I led the team of software engineers, competing in the VRX 2023 competition ([securing 2nd place](https://robotx.org/2023/11/17/vrx-2023-awards-and-final-standings/)) and the MATE ROV competition. My responsibilities also included cross-functional hardware troubleshooting.

**Skills Earned:** Arduino, STM32, Robotics Simulation (Gazebo), ROS2, Linux, Robotics Systems architecture.
